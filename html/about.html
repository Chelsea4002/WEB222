About Author

My name is Hyerang Cho and I am currently studying at Seneca College with a major in Computer Programming and Analysis. Although there were a lot of games I've enjoyed playing, the reason why I chose Blue Marble is that this board game reminds me of good memories with my family and friends. My dad bought this board game for me when I was in elementary school. On the day I got it, I played Blue Marble with my dad, mom, and brother for over an hour. Also, whenever my friends came over to my place, we had fun playing this board game. Playing Blue Marble still brings me joys so I wanted to introduce this.
